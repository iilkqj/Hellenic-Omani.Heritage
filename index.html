📖 Shared Words

sandoúki (σαντούκι) from Arabic "sanduq" ❤️ kardia (καρδιά) similar to "qalb" (heart)
Also: karati (قيراط), caravan (قافلة), ladi (زيت)

🎨 Decorative Arts Fusion

Greek key meander meets Omani floral and geometric patterns in jewelry and ceramics.

🎵 Folk Music

🇴🇲 Omani Al-ʿArḍa (drums, swords, poetry) | 🇬🇷 Greek Sirtaki (Zorba's dance) — both express courage and hospitality.

🤝 Intangible Heritage

Omani Qahwa (coffee with cardamom & frankincense) 🤝 Greek Philoxenia (love of strangers).

🪡 Traditional Crafts

Silver Khanjar (Oman) & Greek painted pottery / marble carving.
